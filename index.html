About Us

Greenskies Analytics works with risk management professionals to automate processes, analyze data, and provide wisdom from your data. Our services can benefit any organization or individual looking to gain efficiencies in their risk mitigation approach and increased coverage over their risks.

  • Risk Consulting
  • IT General Controls Design and Testing
